About us

This site was realized within the framework of an educational project, of realization of web site in the BUT MMI of Montbéliard.

The imposed subject was "From Amazons to Balancetonporcs, the faces of feminism".

Despite a lack of knowledge and culture prior to the implementation of the project, we hope that the work of research and writing have been enough to fill or arouse your curiosity when the fields of feminism.

If you have any comments to help us improve our site, you can send them to us in the contact tab.

We thank you for your visit and hope to see you soon.
